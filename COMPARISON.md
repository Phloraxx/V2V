## 🌟 Why V2V is Transformative

### 1. **Prevents vs Reacts**
- **V2V**: Predicts and prevents problems before they occur
- **Non-V2V**: Can only react after problems start

### 2. **Coordination vs Isolation**
- **V2V**: All vehicles work together as a system
- **Non-V2V**: Each vehicle operates independently

### 3. **Intelligence vs Rules**
- **V2V**: AI-optimized decisions in real-time
- **Non-V2V**: Static rules and driver judgment

### 4. **Proactive vs Passive**
- **V2V**: Actively optimizes traffic flow
- **Non-V2V**: Passively follows traffic
